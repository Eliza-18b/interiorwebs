Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Butter: 1 cup (2 sticks), softened
  • Sugar: 1 cup granulated sugar
  • Brown Sugar: 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • Eggs: 2 large eggs
  • Vanilla Extract: 2 teaspoons
  • All-Purpose Flour: 3 cups
  • Baking Soda: 1 teaspoon
  • Hot Water: 2 teaspoons
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Chocolate chips: 2 cups (semisweet or your preferred type)
  • Nuts (optionnal): 1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Baking Instructions

    1.Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2.Mix dry ingredients: In a small bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
    3.Cream butter and sugars: In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy.
    4.Add eggs: Beat in the eggs one at a time, ensuring each is well incorporated.
    5.Combine mixtures: Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, beating until well blended.
    6.Add chocolate chips and nuts:Stir in the chocolate chips and nuts (if using).
    7.Drop dough: Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough onto ungreased baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
    8.Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 9 to 11 minutes, or until golden brown.
    9.Cool: Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring them to wire racks to cool completely.
    Enjoy our tasty homemade like cookies!
